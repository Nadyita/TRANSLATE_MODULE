To translate a word sentence from one language into another:
<highlight><tab><symbol>translate 'from'..'to' 'text'<end>
From German to Norse:
<highlight><tab><symbol>translate de..no Heute ist ein schöner Tag<end>
From Norse to French
<highlight><tab><symbol>translate no..fr Jeg elsker Spekkhogger<end>
From English to Spanish
<highlight><tab><symbol>translate en..es What's the weather like in Madrid?<end>

<highlight><tab><symbol>translate 'from' 'text'<end>
To translate from German:
<highlight><tab><symbol>translate de Heute ist ein schöner Tag<end>
To translate from Norse:
<highlight><tab><symbol>translate no Jeg elsker Spekkhogger<end>

<highlight><tab><symbol>translate eo Rikoltanto<end>

To translate a word or sentence from any language unto English,
trying to guess the source language:
<highlight><tab><symbol>translate 'text'<end>
